Our Story

A Journey of Authentic Flavors and Family Traditions

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010 by Chef Safayet Hossain, DesiSpice started as a small family-run restaurant in the heart of the city. With recipes passed down through generations, we've grown into a beloved destination for authentic Indian cuisine.

Our commitment to using traditional cooking methods and the freshest ingredients has remained unchanged since day one.

Our Journey

2010

Grand Opening

DesiSpice opened its doors with just 10 tables and a dream to share authentic Indian flavors.

2014

First Expansion

Doubled our seating capacity and added a tandoor oven for fresh-baked naan.

2018

Best Indian Restaurant Award

Recognized by Food & Culture Magazine for excellence in traditional cuisine.

2023

Modern Renovation

Completely redesigned our space while maintaining our authentic culinary roots.
